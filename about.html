About Us

At SimplyTasks, we believe in the power of simplicity and efficiency to help you stay organised and productive. Founded by small team of students at the University of Denver in 2024, SimplyTasks began as a small class project. Recognising the need for a straightforward yet powerful too to manage hectic schedules, we set out to develop a solution that combines the functionality of a calendar with the convenience of a to-do list in one intuitive platform.
